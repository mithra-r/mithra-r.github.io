About

Aerospace engineer with hands-on expertise in FEM/FEA, composite structural optimization, and structural verification across flight-critical systems. My master's thesis, conducted in collaboration with DLR Braunschweig, focused on characterizing realizable lamination parameter domains for practical composite layups — bridging the gap between mathematical optimization and manufacturable composite designs. At TU Munich, I worked on topology optimization of structural components and structural verification under CS-22 airworthiness standards. Proficient in GFEM and DFEM modelling using HyperWorks, Abaqus CAE, and Ansys, and have validated FEM against destructive physical test data for Ti6Al4V, directly replicating Full Scale Test (FST) correlation workflows.
