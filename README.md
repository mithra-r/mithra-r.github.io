## Projects

### 01 · Master's Thesis — DLR Braunschweig / TU Munich (2024–2025)
**Characterizing Lamination Parameters Realizable Domain for Practical Composite Layups**
- Bi-level optimisation framework for CFRP panels using Classical Lamination Theory
- Python subroutines interfacing with DLR Lightworks for composite panel FEA
- **Result:** 69.3% safety factor mismatch reduction (tension); 100% elimination for coupled shear/compression load cases

### 02 · Semester Thesis SA0109 — TU Munich (Apr–Oct 2023)
**Quantifying Performance Loss of Pre-Set Parameters in Topology Optimisation for AM**
- SIMP-based topology optimisation across 4 benchmark cases (MBB beam, cantilever, tensile, L-crank)
- Custom TO engine built in Python and MATLAB; parametric study of aspect ratio vs compliance
- **Result:** AR < 2 keeps performance loss ≤ 5%; AR = 5 increases loss to 10–25%

### 03 · HiWi Research — TU Munich (Jul 2022–Mar 2025)
**Structural Analysis, Topology Optimisation & Ti6Al4V AM Material Validation**
- L-shaped bell crank topology optimisation under CS-22 airworthiness standards (AM constraints)
- FEA of HAP3 aircraft brackets: 4 failure criteria (von Mises, Drucker-Prager, Hill 48, Cuntze-Bold)
- Ti6Al4V coupon testing (ASTM E8/B831/E9) and validation against HSB allowables
- Shaft dimensioning, system load case definition, CFRP–AM bolt connection analysis
- **Result:** −77% mass, −99% deflection vs reference; AM material meets/exceeds HSB allowables

### 04 · Bachelor's Thesis — RVCE Bangalore (2020–2021)
**Influence of Lattice Parameters on Fatigue Life of 18% Nickel Maraging Steel**
- XRD analysis using Williamson-Hall method to extract crystallite size and microstrain
- High cycle fatigue (HCF) testing under rotating bending; S-N curve generation
- **Result:** Optimal ageing window identified; smaller crystallite size correlates with improved HCF life

### 05 · Minor Project — RVCE Bangalore (2020–2021)
**Shape Optimisation of Drain Holes in a Gas Turbine Shaft (Inconel 718)**
- Parametric FEA study of drain hole geometries (circular, elliptical, rectangular) in Abaqus CAE
- Mesh generation in HyperMesh; CAD modelling in CATIA V5
- **Result:** Rectangular hole with AR 2–2.5 yields minimum stress concentration factor (SCF)
